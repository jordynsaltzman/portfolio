About

Hello, I'm Jordyn. I am a recent graduate from Towson University with a B.S. in Economics. Since June 2019, I have been working as a Digital Account Manager at AltaVista Strategic Partners, a firm specializing in marketing services for contractors.

Outside of work, I am passionate about travel and learning new languages. I am fluent in Spanish and speak Mandarin Chinese at an intermediate level. In each place I visit, I try to learn as much of the local language that I can. I am a certified ESL, or English as a Second Language instructor, having taught at several schools as well as the Esperanza Center in Baltimore. Currently, I am taking this skill to a new level by trying to master programming languages at the Coding Boot Camp at Johns Hopkins University. I am excited to take the plunge into a new field that marries my love of languages, design, and critical thinking.
